The Parcels Framework & Vision

  • Parcels vision
    • Simulating the interaction of physical and biological entities with(in) the ocean
    • Simulating interaction between different oceanic entities, be they active or passive
    • Simulation done by user-defined kernel functions
    • Provide elementary functions as starting point (e.g. advection; deletion; diffusion)
    • Work with as many different OGCM formats as possible
    • Be as efficient has possible while being fully customizable
